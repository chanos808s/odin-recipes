Cheese burger

A classic Cheese burger

To make a delicious cheese burger, it is important that we season our ground beef and stuff moneterey jack cheese inside our patty.Instead of using mayonnaise for the buns, a thousand island dressing will ne used instead. This dressing giving the burger a better taste. Tomatoes and onions will be critical in the process of making a good burger so be ready to slice.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • eggs
  • hamburger buns
  • tomato
  • onion
  • ketchup and mayonnaise to make thousand island
  • unsalted butter
  • salt, pepper and garlic powder

Steps

  1. Add all of the ground beef. Use a large spoon to break it up, and then mix in the eggs. Mix until the eggs are fully incorporated to bring the ground beef mixture together.
  2. .Roll each section into a ball, flatten into a patty, and then place onto the parchment paper. Create a well in the middle of each patty, divide the cheese, and then press it into the middle of the patty.
  3. Slice tomatoes and onions
  4. Pre heat the pan. Then add some olive oil. Add salt and pepper to your patties
  5. Place the patties on the pan
    6. Prepare the buns by seasoning them with oil, salt, and pepper, and toast both sides of each bun on the grill.
  6. Begin preparing your thousand island dressing by adding ketchup and mayonnaise to a cup. Mix that up and a nice, reddish color should appear.
  7. while the patties are almost done, add a piece of unsalted butter and baste the patty with the butter.
  8. Start adding thousand island dressing to your patties.Place your patty on top of the bun. Then begin to add your lettuce, tomatoes and onions
